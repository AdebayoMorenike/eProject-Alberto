Hello!

I'm Tony and I designed this page to write about watches.

On my 27th birthday, watching Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness, Benedict Cumberbatch finished repairing his Jaeger-Lecoultre Master Ultra Thin Perpetual watch. I decided to purchase my first mechanical watch. One day I visited Ximen which is Taipei's entertainment district. Expecting to explore brands like Tissot and Seiko, maybe Citizen but I was introduced to Orient. Orient? I've never heard of it but saw a few amazing watches. Eventually, I grew curious and researched about Orient and grew aware of its popularity for affordable watches, dive or dress watches, and Orient Star.
